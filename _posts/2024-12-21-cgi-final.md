<div class="lang-en">This work, titled 'cgi final', is part of the supernihil archive extracted from YouTube. It is categorized as video and examines the systemic boundaries of the medium. The documentation is sober and objective, focusing on the underlying processes. The work serves as a digital testimony to a unique intervention in the supernihil network. The project values the raw materiality of the medium over conventional aesthetics.</div>
